Ready for a trip?

​

Tokyo- experience the whole breadth of Japanese arts and culture. Centuries-old forms of performing arts still play on stages and sumo tournaments draw crowds. Every spring, Tokyoites head outside to appreciate the cherry blossoms,a tradition older than the city itself.

Paris- countless other gorgeous landmarks and monuments that add to the beauty of the spacious boulevards and their charming cafés. French cuisine is also world-famous, and you can find some of the best of it in Paris.

Bangkok- Vibrant and exciting city rich in history and culture. The Thai capital is known to locals as Krung Thep, the 'City of Angels', and a few days in this exciting city is a must on any Thailand holiday.

London- there's nothing quite like the experience of hopping on a red double-decker bus and seeing some of the most recognisable sights in the world from its top deck.

Seoul- the bustling street-food stalls, lively night markets and world-class restaurants are all jam-packed with delicious local dishes, all made using the freshest of ingredients. Given Seoul's location, it is easy to combine this exciting city with any of our other favourite spots in Asia.

Cairo- a great place to explore Egyptian history and culture. Even though Cairo is an ancient city; but it is also a modern metropolis. If you are visiting Egypt then Cairo must be on your Itinerary.
